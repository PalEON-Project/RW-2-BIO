Methods: 4 mapped fixed radius plots were sampled. Within 16m of plot center, all trees >= 10cm diameter were cored twice. 2 plots were located at Rush Lake (HMC-Rush Lake) and 2 plots at HMC-West. Each tree corresponds with permanent remeasurement plots measured by Kerry Woods. 
 
Publications: Dye, A. 2018. Annual aboveground biomass growth of temperate forests in eastern North America. PhD Dissertation, West Virginia University. 

People involved with sampling/lab analysis: Alex Dye, Ruth Stetler, Lynsey Blackburn, Jonathon Suite

File HMCRL.field.data.csv and HMCWest.field.data: Field data sheet for sampled plots. 
	PlotID: 3 letter site code (HMC) + plot number (1,2,3,4)
	Tree: Numbered tree in plot. 
	Site: Huron Mt. Club
	Species: 4 letter species code
		TSCA: Tsuga canadensis (eastern hemlock)
		ACRU: Acer rubrum (red maple)
		ACSA: Acer saccharin
		THOC: Thuja occidentalis 
		BEAL: Betula alleghaniensis
		POGR: Populus grandiflora
		TIAM: Tilia americana

	Canopy: Canopy position of tree.
		D: Dominant
		C: Co-dominant
		I: Intermediate
		S: Suppressed
	Status: 
		Li: Living
		Sn: Snag
		Lo: Log
	DBH: Diameter at breast height, cm
	Distance: distance in meters from plot center
	Azimuth: azimuth from plot center
	x: lateral coordinates for tree spatial position from plot center calculated from 
		distance and azimuth
	y: longitudinal coordinates for tree spatial position from plot center calculated 		from distance and azimuth

Folder HMCRL_rwl and HMCW_rwl: Contains rwl files by species named as SITE_SPECIES, e.g. HMCRL_TSCA for HMC Rush Lake hemlock. Individual trees are named as PlotID + Tree, e.g. 
		HMC1001 for HMC Plot 1, Tree 1.Plots 2 and 4 are at site HMC Rush Lake and 
		plots 1 and 3 are at HMC West.
